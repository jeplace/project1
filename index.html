Fallout

Fallout is a post-apocalyptic role-playing video game series created by Interplay Entertainment and published by Bethesda Softworks. The series is set in a world devastated by nuclear war, where players navigate through a wasteland filled with mutants, raiders, and other dangers. The games are known for their open-world exploration, roleplaying elements, and dark humor. This website will give a brief background as well as show off some of the games in the series.
